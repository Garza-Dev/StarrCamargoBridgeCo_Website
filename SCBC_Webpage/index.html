Our Mission Statement
"Starr-Camargo Bridge Co. (SCBC) is one of only two international crossings with capacity to handle double 40 ft trailers. As the shortest route to the United States from Central Mexico to Rio Grande City, Texas our mission is to facilitate trade by reducing wait times to ensure the timely and uninterrupted flow of trade."

Why choose us?

Ameriaca’s ports are charged to facilitate growing trade while safeguarding national security. However, increases and changes to global trade have outpaced the ports’ capacity to accommodate them – resulting in congestion and delays. Today’s level of demand exceeds the physical infrastructure and operating capacity of our port and our border crossings are not optimally disbursed to minimize congestion.
